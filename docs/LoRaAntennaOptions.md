# LoRa Antenna Options
[←Top](../README.md)<BR>


A wire antenna, aka "quarter wave whip antenna" is low cost and works very well! You just have to cut the wire to the right length.
| Band       | Region / Country notes                                                  | Antenna length (¼‑wave)      |
|------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------|-------------------------------|
| EU868      | Europe (EU region, e.g., Germany, France, Italy, Spain, etc.)           | 8.6 cm ≈ 3.4 in              |
| US915      | United States (and other countries using 902–928 MHz)                     | 7.9 cm ≈ 3.1 in              |
| AU915      | Australia (and some Pacific neighbors using 915–928 MHz)                  | 7.9 cm ≈ 3.1 in              |
| AS923      | Asia (including Japan, Singapore, Taiwan, Indonesia, etc.)               | 7.6 cm ≈ 3.0 in              |
| KR920      | South Korea (using 920–923 MHz)                                          | 7.8 cm ≈ 3.1 in              |
| IN866      | India (866 MHz band)                                                     | 8.2 cm ≈ 3.2 in              |

Additional information on antenna options can be found at [Adafruit](https://learn.adafruit.com/adafruit-feather-m0-radio-with-lora-radio-module/antenna-options).
